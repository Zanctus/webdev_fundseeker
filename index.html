About us
Fund Seeker is a community of compassionate individuals who believe in the collective power of giving. Through our platform, donors have the opportunity to discover compelling campaigns and contribute to causes that inspire them. We provide a supportive and inclusive space where individuals, non-profits, community projects, and charitable organizations can share their stories and seek the necessary funds to turn their aspirations into reality.
Join us today and be part of a movement. Together, let's make a lasting impact, one donation at a time.

Donator

  1. Click the donate button
  2. Browse and review among the list of donations
  3. Choose the one you want to help with
  4. Contact the person who requested it

Requester

  1. Click the request button
  2. Fill up necessary details
  3. Once finalizing the information, click submit.
  4. Wait for donators to contact you.
